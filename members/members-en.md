# Membership in the Swiss Tablesoccer Federation

As a member of the Swiss Tablesoccer Federation (STF), your club benefits from a wide range of advantages:

- Free tournament entries in the tournament calendar on the STF website
- Annual player license fee of CHF 50.00 instead of CHF 80.00 (when requested via the club before 31.12 of the previous year)
- Increased visibility of your club in the Swiss table soccer scene
- More players at tournaments (additional club revenue)
- Voting rights at the STF delegates' assembly
- Proactive updates from STF regarding regulations and sport framework changes
- Support for marketing and sponsor search (sponsor templates, contract templates)
- Purchasing discounts from our main sponsors (tables, trophies, coaching, promotional material)
- Possibility for your club to participate in the Swiss Tablesoccer League
