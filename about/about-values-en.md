# Our Vision

We connect grassroots and elite sport and stand for a union of game, fun and sport within Swiss table football.

**For our members**

- We pursue constructive and respectful collaboration with our members to achieve our goals together
- We offer a broad platform of straightforward tools for the efficient organisation of tournaments

**For the sport**

- We maintain a professional relationship with the international federation ITSF and other table football federations
- We promote youth development and the recruitment of new members through development programmes
- We engage with organisations for people with disabilities and support the integration of para-sport in table football

**For players**

- Our tournament organisation is fully financed through sponsorship funds
- We are pursuing admission as a member of Swiss Olympic to gain recognition of table football as an Olympic sport
- We provide an appropriate platform for all classifications

# Our Mission

We promote and strengthen table football in Switzerland through collaboration, development and inclusion.

# Our Guiding Principles

The Swiss Tablesoccer Federation (STF) is the national association for table football across all classification levels. Our highest objective is to inspire as many people as possible to be enthusiastic about table football and motivate them to participate actively.

The STF pursues the national and international interests of table football and is committed to para-sport.

An important flagship is the national team selection.

The STF is responsible for regulated competition operations and issues the regulatory foundations for them.

The STF is committed to respectful interaction and combats all forms of discrimination and violence at and around the table, and reserves the right to impose sanctions.

The STF recognises the Swiss Olympic Ethics Charter and follows the ethical principles contained in it.

The STF secures its resources through membership fees, licence revenues and sponsorship contributions. These are used exclusively to promote table football in both elite and grassroots sport.

The STF organises and markets the annual Swiss Championships and the Swiss Tablesoccer League, thereby strengthening collaboration with its members and sponsors.
