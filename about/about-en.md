# About Us
__We are the supreme governing body of Swiss tablesoccer and are committed to the development of the sport in Switzerland.__

The Swiss Tablesoccer Federation (STF) is the umbrella organisation of [Swiss tablesoccer clubs](https://www.swisstablesoccer.ch/mitgliedvereine-stf). It was founded in 2007 and has been a member of the international tablesoccer federation ITSF since its inception. Today the federation counts 24 member clubs and over 1,200 active players.

Since 2018, the Swiss Tablesoccer Federation has been a member of [SwissOlympic](https://www.swissolympic.ch) and is thus officially recognised as a sport in Switzerland.

# Our Vision

We connect grassroots and elite sport and stand for a union of game, fun and sport within Swiss table football.

# Our Mission

We promote and strengthen table football in Switzerland through collaboration, development and inclusion.

# Our Guiding Principles

**For our members**

- We pursue constructive and respectful collaboration with our members to achieve our goals together
- We offer a broad platform of straightforward tools for the efficient organisation of tournaments

**For the sport**

- We maintain a professional relationship with the international federation ITSF and other table football federations
- We promote youth development and the recruitment of new members through development programmes
- We engage with organisations for people with disabilities and support the integration of para-sport in table football

**For players**

- Our tournament organisation is fully financed through sponsorship funds
- We seek admission as a member of Swiss Olympic to secure recognition of table football as an Olympic sport
- We provide an appropriate platform for all classifications
